About Us

Blessings International Foundation is a non-profit organization, that was mainly set up , give assistance and support to the poor, needy, aged, orphaned, homeless and widowed.

Our Mission is to provide unique and meaningful charitable assistance to all in need like school stationaries and uniforms for needy children, hospital supplies and foodstuffs to newly born babies, free pre-natal checkup for pregnant women and good source of water for communities in need of potable water.
